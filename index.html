Translating Nature into Medicine

At Bioinformatics Inc., we harness the complexity of the natural world to tackle today’s biggest
healthcare challenges. With breakthrough advancements in machine learning, computational metabolomics,
knowledge graphs,we’re discovering the next generation of small molecule therapeutics.

Biomedical engineering (BME) is the application of engineering principles to solve
biological and medical problems for the purpose of improving health care. You may be surprised
just how many biomedical technologies you already know. Biomedical engineers created many of today's
standard health care instruments.
